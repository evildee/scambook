Strange But True: Poodle Buyer Discovers His Dog is a Ferret on Steroids

No pedigree? No problem! An incredible scam has been discovered at a market in Argentina, where would-be dog owners have found themselves walking away with a great deal on a poodle that's actually a ferret. Yes, you read that correctly. A ferret. It's one of the most bizarre scams we've come across in a while. The pet scam was sniffed out by an older man who purchased two "toy poodles" and brought them to a vet. The doctor then informed the man, to his surprise, that he was the proud owner of what locals call "Brazilian rats."
