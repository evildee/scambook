Making Money From Home: Why You Should Think Twice

Earning a substantial income with minimal effort is undoubtedly a fantasy that many working adults have played with in their minds at one point or another. Unfortunately, this concept is a premium target that online criminals seek to exploit. The “Make money from home” scheme is a popular topic on Scambook and the most unsettling aspect is how these thieves go about executing this ruse.

Thanks to some detailed user reports, we’ve learned that so many victims were legitimately fooled from the start because they received emails from friends or relatives urging them to sign up to start making money right away.
